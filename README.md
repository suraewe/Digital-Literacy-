### Task 5: Cyber Awareness & Safety

Researched **UPI fraud and cybercrime practices**, focusing on how scammers exploit urgency and user behavior.

**Key Learnings:**

* UPI PIN is only required to **send money**, never to receive
* Scammers use **fake payment requests, QR codes, and urgency tactics**
* College students are common targets despite being tech-savvy

**Prevention Measures:**

* Verify UPI IDs before payments
* Avoid clicking unknown links
* Use strong passwords and enable 2FA
* Avoid transactions on public Wi-Fi

**Emergency Response:**

* Report fraud at: [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* Helpline: **1930**

**Personal Takeaway:**
I will verify all financial requests and avoid trusting unknown links or urgent messages.
